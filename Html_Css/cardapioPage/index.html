Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparetion: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingreddients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs

Instruction

1.Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. you can ad a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

2.Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil

3.Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melte and bubbling, pour in th eggs. tilt th pan to ensure the eggs evenly caat the surface

4.Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middl, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one galf of the omelette

5.Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. let it cook for another minute, then slide ir onte a plate

6.Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if neede
